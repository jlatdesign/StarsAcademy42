Who We Are

More Than a Game

Stars Academy 42 was founded with one clear purpose: give every kid a shot at the diamond. We believe talent has no price tag, and access to quality baseball coaching should never depend on a family's financial situation.

Our certified coaches bring professional-level instruction to every practice, teaching proper mechanics, baseball IQ, and the competitive mindset that turns good players into great ones.

Our Coaching Philosophy

How We Develop Players

Every rep, every drill, and every game is guided by these core principles of player development.

Fundamentals First

We build every player on a rock-solid foundation of proper mechanics — footwork, throwing, fielding, and hitting technique done right from day one.

Baseball IQ

Great players think the game. We teach situational awareness, reading pitchers, defensive positioning, and the mental side of competing at a high level.

Team First

Baseball is the ultimate team sport. We instill selflessness, communication, and the understanding that winning together is always better than individual glory.

Compete Hard

We train with intensity and play with passion. Our athletes learn to embrace competition, handle adversity, and give maximum effort on every single pitch.

Arm Care & Safety

Player health is non-negotiable. We follow strict pitch count guidelines, proper warm-up protocols, and educate every athlete on injury prevention and long-term arm health.
